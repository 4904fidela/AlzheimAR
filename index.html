Design Archetype: Primary Persona

To address the absolute lack of digital literacy, AlzheimAR provides a zero-interaction NUI.

W

Ibu Wati (72 Years Old)

Moderate-Stage Alzheimer's Patient

Cognitive Condition Short-term memory loss, episodic spatial disorientation (sundowning 15:00-18:00), progressive face blindness (prosopagnosia).
Tech Literacy Zero. Cannot operate a smartphone or any device. Interacts only with landline telephones and analogue wall clocks.
Daily Routine Wakes at 05:30. Garden walk 09:00–11:00. Sundowning confusion spikes in the afternoon. Bed by 21:00.

Design Methodology: Stingray Plan

Board of Innovation Framework: Train → Develop → Iterate

01

TRAIN (AI Intensive)

Conducted literature reviews on Alzheimer's AR color psychology. Established calming vs. anxiety-triggering colors and the passive-only interaction hypothesis.

02

DEVELOP (AI + Human)

Designed the emerald arrow path concepts. Defined the cognitive load limit: maximum of 1 AR visual element active on the lens at a time.

03

ITERATE (Human Intensive)

Validated audio-visual sync: bone-conduction audio complements visuals. Set data contracts (JSON schema) for external caregiver synchronizations.

Technical Specifications & Constraints

IEEE Std 830-1998 Compliant

On-Device AI Processing

  • Edge-AI Face Recognition: <3s latency, ≥90% accuracy in typical indoor settings.
  • Voice Spotting: Offline wake-word detection for "Tolong" SOS trigger.
  • No Cloud Streaming: Raw video content is processed entirely locally on the glasses to protect privacy.

Form & Power

  • Form Factor: Conventional eyewear (Meta Ray-Ban style) weighing less than 60g for all-day comfort.
  • Battery: ≥10 hours continuous active NUI overlays, or ≥14 hours in low-activity mode.
  • No Fine-motor Buttons: Single power button located on the charging dock only.

Security & Reliability

  • Offline Resilience: Schedule cached locally for ≥24h. The glasses operate normally even if connectivity is lost.
  • Fail-safe Revert: Hardware errors cause the display to turn off completely, reverting to a normal passive eyewear lens.
  • Data Protection: TLS 1.3 transit encryption, AES-256 for local biometric models.
